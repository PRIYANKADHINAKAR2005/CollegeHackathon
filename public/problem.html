25B3PS006
TRANSFORMODOCS-Machine Readable Document Management System
Description:
In many organizations, documents are often stored in non-machine-readable formats, making it difficult to search, analyze, and extract useful information efficiently. This problem becomes even more challenging when dealing with large volumes of documents in varying formats. The goal of TransformoDocs is to address these challenges by restricting the ingestion of non-machine-readable documents and enabling their seamless conversion to machine-readable formats. The system aims to automate the conversion process using Optical Character Recognition (OCR) technology, ensuring that documents are structured and searchable. Additionally, it integrates AI-based search functionality, enabling semantic searches for better document discoverability. The system also ensures compliance with accessibility standards like WCAG and provides reporting and analytics features for insights into document usage patterns. This solution will improve document accessibility, searchability, and overall efficiency in managing large volumes of documents.
