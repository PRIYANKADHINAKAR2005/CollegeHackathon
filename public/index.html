About BYTEBEAT BRAWL and IPC

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), the CSI Student Chapter, and the Coding Club proudly present BYTEBEAT BRAWL '25 and Idea to Product Challenge (IPC '25), two exhilarating competitions designed to ignite innovation, foster creativity, and challenge participants to develop impactful technological solutions. These events are not just competitions; they are opportunities to showcase your skills, transform ideas into tangible products, solve real-world challenges, and collaborate with fellow tech enthusiasts. Both events encourage participants to create innovative solutions, apply their technical knowledge, and make their mark in the world of technology.

Why BYTEBEAT BRAWL and IPC?

BYTEBEAT represents the rhythm of innovation, while BRAWL and IPC challenge participants to push the boundaries of what’s possible with their ideas, skills, and ingenuity. These events serve as platforms where creativity meets technical expertise, empowering participants to transform visionary concepts into impactful solutions and drive meaningful change
